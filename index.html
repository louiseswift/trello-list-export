Export Lists from Trello

Get the card details from specified Trello lists in a handy copy-and-pasteable format.

First, authenticate with Trello so that we can access your boards, lists and cards in there.

Great! Now you need to decide which board(s) you want to pull lists from.

Grab the board ID's from the URL of the boards and enter them as a comma-separated list into the field below.

(If you're not sure how to find a board's ID, visit that board in your browser and look at the address bar. You should see a URL like https://trello.com/b/aBCdefGh/board-name-here - the bit in red there is the board's ID.)

IMPORTANT: Make sure to add the capitalised word 'EXPORT' to the title of all lists on your chosen board(s) that you want to export!
